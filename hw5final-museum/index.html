KINGDOM HEARTS JET SET RADIO FINAL FANTASY HALO

Tasty-Looking Ice Cream


I never knew what was going on, all I knew this game for was the anime-styled characters fighting alongside random disney characters like Mickey Mouse and Goofy.

I always thought the music was so beautiful, Utada Hikaru is the woman who's behind the opening. I think she's incredibly popular now in the US, it wasn't the case back then until the introduction of KH, i'm glad she gets the attention she deserves.

I remember so clearly, that 'sea-salt' ice cream and I wanted one SO badly. The characters would eat them from time to time, and it was really prominent on the CRT TV, the glistening glow of the blue popsicle.
