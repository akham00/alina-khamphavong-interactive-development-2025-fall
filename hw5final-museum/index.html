KINGDOM HEARTS JET SET RADIO FINAL FANTASY HALO

Tasty-Looking Ice Cream


I never knew what was going on, all I knew this game for was the anime-styled characters fighting alongside random disney characters like Mickey Mouse and Goofy.

I always thought the music was so beautiful, Utada Hikaru is the woman who's behind the opening. I think she's incredibly popular now in the US, it wasn't the case back then until the introduction of KH, i'm glad she gets the attention she deserves.

I remember so clearly, that 'sea-salt' ice cream and I wanted one SO badly. The characters would eat them from time to time, and it was really prominent on the CRT TV, the glistening glow of the blue popsicle.

Funky Beats and Skates


It's still such a favorite game of mine, I ended up playing this when I was old enough to.

I actually tried to learn skating with rollerblades when I was younger because I was so inspired, but...

I kept falling on my butt and I got pretty mad about it and gave up. Maybe I should pick it back up.

Stylish Characters Fighting


There's a character named Lightning, she's stoic, pretty, and has spiky pink hair. I remember vividly that I begged my mom to cut my hair like hers.

It didn't really work out. it's pretty hard to translate into real life.

And I wasn't allowed to have pink hair at that age.

Despite my attachment to that character, I have idea what this game is about, to be honest, I still don't really know. I'm just kind of aware it's a fighting game? The storyline seems pretty complicated. Maybe I should play this now that I'm older.

Shooting Colorful Aliens

I remember this being one of the few games that I was able to play with my brother, because it was couch-op. I unfortunately don't think I was a very helpful teammate when I was 8 years old.
